Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 31, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Refiner collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Refiner mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Email address and display name
  • Profile photo (if provided)
  • Journal entries you create
  • Feedback submissions and screenshots (if added)
  • App version, device type, and operating system

How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain app functionality
  • To sync user data across devices
  • To improve reliability and user experience
  • To review feedback and improve the app

Data Storage

Refiner uses Google Firebase services (Authentication, Firestore, and Storage) to securely store user data.

Account & Data Deletion

Users can delete their account and associated data directly within the app. Journal entries can also be deleted individually.

Third-Party Services

Refiner relies on Firebase services provided by Google LLC.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. Continued use of the app means acceptance of the updated policy.
